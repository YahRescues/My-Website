Allah makes Jesus’ followers superior

We see in the Quran verses that Allah will make the followers of Jesus dominant ‘until the day of resurrection’. Either Allah was wrong, or Paul, and Constantine were made to succeed and be dominant by the help of Allah. We see clearly from Ibn Kathir that the religion of Constantine became superior. We would also have to say that Allah caused Paul to become superior with his message. If the true followers of Jesus were the ones who just thought he was a prophet, and a slave to Allah, and Allah was going to make them superior until the day of resurrection, then Muhammad wouldn’t have needed to correct anything of the Christians. He would be correcting the work of Allah who made the true believers dominant. For example, he couldn’t have come against the dominance of the trinity. The question then becomes why did Allah make Paul and Constantine superior to those who deemed Jesus (Isa) to be only a prophet? Why did Allah make Constantine superior in changing the religion of Jesus? Out of the 4 beliefs Ibn Kathir mentions the one which became dominant was the Trinity. Why does Allah make the trinitarians dominant? By saying that the religion of Jesus became the religion of Constantine, Ibn Kathir is conceding that Constantine was made dominant. Qushairi made it clear, he says there was a group of believers who believed in what Jesus really taught in his declaration of the Islamic idea of the oneness of God. These would be made superior until the day of resurrection.
